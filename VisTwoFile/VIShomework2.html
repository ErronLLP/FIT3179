The Index

The annual European Backpacker Index, compiled by priceoftravel.com, is designed to help money saving travellers find the cheapest European cities and plan an accurate budget for when they visit.

Prices for most things like hotel, transportation and attractions are fixed and certain.However, prices for a 'budget lunch' or a pint of beer can vary depending on where you go. The index takes all of these factors into account to calculate the approximate price of a day in each city, inlcuding an overnight stay at a backpacker hostel.
