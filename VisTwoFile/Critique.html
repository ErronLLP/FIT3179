Introduction

The Australian Bushfires 2019-2020 refers to a series of hill fires that will start burning across Australia in September 2019 due to hot and dry weather. The University of Sydney estimates that over one billion animals have been killed by the ongoing fires across the country.

This visualisation of the Australian bushfires in Australia in August 2019 has been selected to provide insight into future fire prevention. Brightness temperature 21 (Kelvin): Channel 21/22 brightness temperature of the fire pixel measured in Kelvin.

300 Kelvin = 26.85 celsius

Formula: 300K − 273.15 = 26.85°C
